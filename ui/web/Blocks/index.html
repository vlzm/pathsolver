How to Play Blocks

Place falling blocks to create complete horizontal lines. When a line is completed, it disappears and you earn points!

Controls

  • ← → or A/D - Move left/right
  • ↓ or S - Soft drop (faster fall)
  • ↑ or W/X - Rotate clockwise
  • Z or Ctrl - Rotate counter-clockwise
  • Space - Hard drop (instant fall)
  • C or Shift - Hold piece
  • P or Esc - Pause game

Touch Controls

  • Tap board - Hard drop
  • Swipe down - Soft drop
  • Arrow buttons - Move left/right
  • Rotate button - Rotate clockwise
  • Double-tap rotate - Rotate counter-clockwise
  • Tap Hold box - Hold piece

Mouse Controls (when enabled)

  • Move mouse - Position piece
  • Left click - Hard drop
  • Right click - Hold piece
  • Middle click - Rotate clockwise
  • Scroll wheel - Rotate piece

Scoring System

  • Single - 100 × level
  • Double - 300 × level
  • Triple - 500 × level
  • Tetris - 800 × level

Advanced Techniques

  • T-Spin - Rotate a T-piece into a tight spot (400-1600 points)
  • Mini T-Spin - Smaller T-Spin variant (100-400 points)
  • Back-to-Back - Chain Tetrises or T-Spins for 1.5× points
  • Combo - Clear lines consecutively for bonus points
  • Perfect Clear - Clear the entire board for huge bonus!

Tips

  • Use the ghost piece to see where your piece will land
  • Hold pieces strategically to set up big clears
  • T-Spins are worth more than regular line clears
  • Keep your stack low and flat for more options
  • Practice wall kicks to fit pieces in tight spots
